RM (Kim Namjoon)

The Philosopher Who Raps

Who is RM?

Kim Namjoon, widely known by his stage name RM, is the leader of the global sensation BTS. He is celebrated not only as a rapper, songwriter, and producer but also as a thinker and cultural bridge. Fluent in Korean and English, RM blends intellectual depth with artistic brilliance, making him one of the most influential figures of his generation.

“Namjoon takes care of and is very good to every single one of the members... When the members have worries, he speaks with them personally, giving advice and reminders.”
— Kim Taehyung (V) on RM’s leadership

RM’s Artistry and Lyricism

RM is a master lyricist whose works touch on self-love, mental health, social justice, and philosophy. His words carry weight because they are raw, thoughtful, and deeply personal. RM doesn’t shy away from difficult subjects, making his lyrics a source of comfort and inspiration worldwide.

"RM is a cultural reset of modern-day songwriting. His lyrics aren’t just flowers and butterflies; they are reality checks, a reflection of all the struggles and possibilities of life."
— Jc on Medium (2020)

Achievements and Records

>   As a Leader and BTS Member

  • Guided BTS to become the first Korean act to top the Billboard Hot 100.
  • Led BTS’s UN General Assembly speeches (2018, 2021), inspiring global youth.
  • Spoke at the White House in 2022 against anti-Asian hate crimes.
  • Billboard #1 albums with BTS, making history as global icons.

>   As a Solo Artist

  • Indigo (2022) — RM’s debut solo album, charted on the Billboard 200 for over 20 weeks, a first for a Korean soloist.
  • Collaborations with Anderson .Paak, Erykah Badu, Colde, and more, showcasing his global artistry.
  • Praised for blending Korean identity with international sounds.

>   Honours and Recognition

  • Youngest and 2nd most-credited songwriter in KOMCA history (200+ songs).
  • Artnet Innovators List (2022) for his contributions to global art and culture.
  • Apollo’s “40 Under 40 Asia Pacific” (2022) for his influence on art collecting.
  • Honorary Ambassador for Korea’s Ministry of National Defense Excavation Agency (2022).
  • First and only celebrity ambassador for Bottega Veneta (2023).
  • Featured in Rolling Stone’s “200 Greatest Singers of All Time” (2023, with BTS).

The Genius Mind: IQ 148

RM’s IQ of 148 places him in the top 0.1% of the global population. This means his cognitive ability is higher than 99.9% of people worldwide. As a student, he ranked in the top 1% nationwide in Korea for language, mathematics, and social studies. His intellectual ability, combined with creativity, makes him a truly unique artist.

"In addition to being a language whiz, he’s also a human calculator."
— Koreaboo

Self-taught in English through the sitcom Friends, RM later took the TOEIC exam “just for fun” and scored an impressive 915/990.

Impact on Youth and Culture

RM’s words and actions go beyond music. His advocacy for self-acceptance, resilience, and unity has touched millions of fans. By encouraging young people to embrace their identity and flaws, he has become a symbol of hope and authenticity. His influence extends into literature, art, and global culture.

"BTS RM is not only influencing youth through music but also by fostering a love of art and culture."
— Ahona Mitra

Legacy and Ongoing Influence

RM’s journey reflects dedication, resilience, and vision. From leading BTS to breaking records as a solo artist, he has redefined what it means to be a global musician. His influence continues to inspire musicians, artists, thinkers, and fans across the world. RM is not just a rapper or leader; he is a cultural icon shaping the present and future.
